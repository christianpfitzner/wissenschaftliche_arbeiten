        ## TH Nürnberg Georg Simon Ohm

        **Technische Hochschule Nürnberg Georg Simon Ohm** (OHM) is one of the largest
        universities of applied sciences in Germany.

        - [TH Nürnberg — Home](https://www.th-nuernberg.de/)
        - [Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Precision Engineering and Information Technology (efi)](https://www.th-nuernberg.de/fakultaeten/efi/)
        - [Mobile Robotics Lab](https://www.th-nuernberg.de/fakultaeten/efi/forschung/forschungsaktive-labore/mobile-robotik/)

        ## TTZ Nürnberger Land

        The **Technology Transfer Centre Nürnberger Land** (TTZ) is a joint facility of
        TH Nürnberg and the Nürnberger Land district. Several of the thesis topics posted
        here are conducted at TTZ in Lauf a. d. Pegnitz.

        - [TTZ Nürnberger Land](https://www.th-nuernberg.de/einrichtungen-gesamt/wissenschaftliche-und-forschungskooperationen/technologietransferzentrum-nuernberger-land/)

        ## AerodrOHM — Drone Research at OIC

        **AerodrOHM** is the drone research group of TH Nürnberg, based at the
        OHM Innovation Campus (OIC). Research focuses on autonomous aerial systems,
        drone swarms, and UAV-based sensing.

        - [AerodrOHM — OHM Innovation Campus](https://www.th-nuernberg.de/einrichtungen-gesamt/einrichtungen-a-z/ohm-innovation-campus-oic/)

        ---

        ## Team AutonOHM

        **AutonOHM** is TH Nürnberg's team for autonomous mobile robots.
        The team participates in national and international competitions and
        conducts research in autonomous navigation, manipulation and human-robot interaction.

        - [AutonOHM — Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/autonomohm/)
        - [AutonOHM — GitHub](https://github.com/autonohm)
