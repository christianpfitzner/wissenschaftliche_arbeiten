abstract: |
  The aim of this case study is to survey primarily small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs)
  in the Nürnberger Land region regarding their use of new technologies such as artificial
  intelligence, mobile and stationary robotic systems, and the potential for automating
  tasks and processes.

  This includes identifying technologies already in use, assessing willingness to adopt
  new technologies in production, quality management and technical communication, and
  understanding the challenges and barriers involved. Surveys are to be methodically
  prepared, suitable companies identified, and decision-makers interviewed and evaluated.

  The result is a summarising presentation of the findings and a brief assessment of
  potentially relevant technologies for this sector.

## About the TTZ Nürnberger Land

The Technology Transfer Centre Nürnberger Land aims to advance knowledge transfer from research
into primarily small and medium-sized enterprises in the Nürnberger Land region. The centre
maintains a broad network of companies in this region.

## Your Tasks

- Methodical preparation and execution of company surveys
- Identification of suitable companies in the Nürnberger Land region
- Conducting interviews with decision-makers
- Evaluation and summarising presentation of the results
- Classification of relevant technologies (AI, robotics, automation) for the sector

## Requirements

- Interest in case studies
- Broad technical background
- Communication skills
- Organisational ability
- Independent and goal-oriented working style
